Regulators spoil Ant’s party less than 48 hours before it starts trading

Ant agonistes 蚂蚁激动剂

#监管机构在蚂蚁集团开始交易前不到48小时就破坏了它

The world’s biggest IPO is now the world’s biggest suspended IPO
全球最大IPO是目前全球最大的暂停IPO

Spoil 毁掉

Spoil one’s chances of getting/doing sth 断送自己获得某事物/做某事的机会

Nov 3rd 2020

JACK MA WAS in a triumphant mood shortly after Ant Group, his Chinese fintech firm, priced its initial public offering—set to be the world’s biggest ever, with almost $40bn worth of shares sold. Speaking at a summit in Shanghai on October 24th, he chided regulators for being too focused on preventing financial risks. Red tape, he said, only held up innovation. Ten days later his words came back to haunt him. Less than 48 hours before its stock was to begin trading in Hong Kong and Shanghai, Ant was forced by Chinese regulators to halt the flotation.

shortly after …之后立即

initial public offering 首次公开发行

Chide 斥责

Triumphant 心满意足的；欢欣鼓舞的

Red tape 繁文缛节

Hold up 延误；阻碍

Come back to sth 回到某事

马云（jackma）的中国金融科技公司蚂蚁集团（antgroup）将其首次公开发行（ipo）定为全球有史以来规模最大的一次，出售了价值近400亿美元的股票，马云当时心情大好。在10月24日于上海举行的一次峰会上，他指责监管机构过于注重防范金融风险。他说，繁文缛节只会阻碍创新。十天后，他的话又萦绕在他心头。在股票在香港和上海开始交易不到48小时之前，监管机构就强迫蚂蚁停止上市。

The group said in a regulatory notice to the Hong Kong exchange that the IPO, scheduled for November 5th, had been suspended because the company “may not meet listing qualifications or disclosure requirements”, after the regulator conducted an interview with Mr Ma and other executives. The filings also mentioned “recent changes in the fintech regulatory environment”, hinting that newly published rules may have got in the way. The sudden suspension also suggests that some powerful officials may be displeased with Mr Ma, a self-made man who, by the conservative standards of big business in China, has an outspoken streak.

in the way 阻碍，挡道

Self-made 靠自己奋斗成功的；白手起家的

Outspoken 直言不讳的；公开的

Streak 性格特征

Have a streak of sth in one’s character 性格中有某种特点

该集团在香港交易所的监管公告中表示，由于监管机构对马云和其他高管进行了采访，该公司预计将于11月5日暂停IPO，因为该公司“可能无法满足上市资格或披露要求”。文件中还提到了“金融科技监管环境最近的变化”，暗示新发布的规则可能已经成为阻碍。突然停止也表明，一些有权势的officials可能对马云感到不满。按照中国大企业的保守标准，马云是一个白手起家的人，他有直言不讳的性格。

Yet the turn of events is not just painful for Ant. It reflects poorly on China’s regulators. IPOs are rarely stopped at such a late stage. The deal was more than 800 times oversubscribed in Shanghai, and in Hong Kong last week the firm closed its book a day early. It was set to float in Shanghai on the STAR market, China’s answer to Nasdaq, designed to lure home Chinese tech groups that have listed abroad. Instead, the last-minute halt of Ant’s listing highlights the opacity of the Chinese political system and the risks that can trip up even its most successful companies.

Oversubscribed 超额订购的

STAR market 科创板

trip up 绊倒 ; 绊 ; 使绊 ; 将…绊倒 ; 使跌倒 ; 犯错 ; 失误

然而，事态的转变对蚂蚁来说不仅仅是痛苦的。它对中国监管机构的反映不佳。ipo在如此晚的阶段很少会停止。这笔交易在上海超额认购超过800倍，上周在香港，该公司提前一天就结清了账目。它将在上海星空市场（starmarket）上市，这是中国对纳斯达克（Nasdaq）的回应，旨在吸引在海外上市的中国科技集团。相反，蚂蚁在最后一刻停止上市，突显出political体系的不透明性，以及即便是最成功的公司，也有可能陷入困境的风险。

It is also easily the most public, and most disruptive, of Ant’s run-ins with regulators. The company has consistently adjusted its business as the rules around it have shifted. The IPO debacle appears to be partly related to one such case. Back in 2018 officials placed caps on asset-backed securitization, upending Ant’s model of selling its loans on to banks. So it pioneered a new approach: consumers and merchants borrowed through Alipay, its payments service, on their smartphones, but the money came from banks. Ant was simply the conduit, collecting a “technology-service fee”. This conduit business boomed, with credit growing to outstrip payments as Ant’s biggest source of revenue.

run-ins 争论 ; 争吵 ; 冲突 ;

Disruptive 引起混乱的

Securitization 证券化

asset-backed securitization 资产支持证券化

Conduit 渠道

Outstrip 超过

这也很容易成为蚂蚁与监管者之间最公开、最具破坏性的一次。随着周围规则的改变，该公司一直在调整业务。IPO的失败似乎在一定程度上与此类案例有关。早在2018年，政府就对资产支持证券化设置了上限，颠覆了蚂蚁银行向银行出售贷款的模式。因此，它开创了一种新的方法：消费者和商家通过支付宝（Alipay）的智能手机借款，但这些钱来自银行。蚂蚁只是管道，收取“技术服务费”。这一渠道业务蓬勃发展，信贷增长超过支付成为蚂蚁最大的收入来源。

On November 2nd the Chinese central bank and banking regulator published new draft rules for online micro-lending, which looked almost perfectly tailored to undercut Ant. Online lenders will need to fund at least 30% of any loan they supply jointly with banks. That could force Ant to keep much more of the credit that it originates on its books; currently, 98% is held as assets by other firms, off Ant’s balance-sheet. Additional disclosure requirements could also make it much more cumbersome for banks to partner with the group. Officials at China’s banking regulator have already pressed commercial lenders to adhere to the new rules, according to Bloomberg, in effect making many of Ant’s transactions non-compliant.

adhere to 坚持，遵守，附着于

in effect 其实;实际上

11月2日，中国央行和银行业监管机构公布了新的网上小额贷款规则草案，该草案看起来几乎完全是为“削弱蚂蚁”量身定做的。在线贷款机构将需要为他们与银行联合提供的任何贷款的至少30%提供资金。这可能会迫使Ant将其产生的信贷更多地记在账上；目前, 98%的信贷被其他公司作为资产持有，不在Ant的资产负债表内。额外的披露要求也可能使银行与该集团合作更加麻烦。彭博社（Bloomberg）称，银行业监管机构的官员已经向商业银行施压，要求它们遵守新规定，这实际上使蚂蚁的许多交易不合规。

Depending on how the rules are implemented—they will not be finalized until December—Ant’s capital-light model could end up looking much less sleek. “It will be valued more like a financial firm than a tech firm,” said a strategist with an Asian sovereign-wealth fund. Given Ant’s track record, it seems a fair bet that it will quickly adapt to the new rules, but its credit operations are likely to face much slower growth and lower profitability.

Finalize 最后确定

capital-light model 资本轻量化模型

It’s a good bet that 很有可能..

Bet 推测

根据规则的实施方式，这些规则将在12月前最终确定，蚂蚁的资本轻量化模型可能最终看起来不那么圆滑。一家亚洲主权财富基金的策略师表示：“它的估值将更像是一家金融公司，而不是一家科技公司。” 考虑到蚂蚁的历史记录，它很可能会很快适应新规则，但其信贷业务可能会面临增长放缓和盈利能力下降的局面。

Investors had previously given Ant a forward price-to-earnings multiple of 40, in line with big global payments companies. Most Chinese banks, in contrast, trade on multiples of less than ten. Ant had been on track for a market capitalization north of $300bn, higher than any bank in the world. Now it is likely to fall well short of that. Shares in Alibaba, a Chinese e-commerce giant that owns a third of Ant, tumbled by 8% after the suspension of the listing was announced.

price-to-earnings 市盈率

multiples 倍数 ;

capitalisation 投资；资本化；市值；资本市值

此前，投资者已经给予蚂蚁一个40倍的远期收益率，与全球大型支付公司一致。相比之下，大多数中国银行的交易倍数不到10倍。

蚂蚁一直在以3000亿美元的市场资本化为目标，高于世界上任何一家银行。

[此前，蚂蚁金服的市值有望突破3000亿美元，高于世界上任何一家银行。]

现在很可能会远远低于这个。阿里巴巴是中国电子商务巨头，拥有蚂蚁三分之一的股份，在宣布暂停上市后，股价下跌8%。

Some investors may at least be grateful to regulators for introducing the new rules before the IPO, however late in the process, allowing them to reprice Ant before they buy its shares. But it is not as if the IPO came as a surprise or the company was an unknown entity; regulators could have acted far earlier. The possibility that they were motivated by a grudge against Mr Ma, and perhaps irritated by his recent speech, cannot be ruled out.

Grudge 积怨

一些投资者至少应该感谢监管者在IPO之前推出了新规定，不管这一过程有多晚，允许他们在购买其股票之前重新定价。但这并不是说IPO来得突然，也不是公司是一个未知的实体；监管机构本可以更早采取行动。他们的动机可能是对马英九的怨恨，也许是对马云最近的讲话感到恼火，这一点不能排除。

Ant had gone to great lengths to brand itself as a tech firm, not a bank. It describes its business as “techfin”—ie, putting technology first—not fintech. In the lead-up to its listing, it asked brokerages to assign tech analysts, not just banking analysts, to cover it. Ultimately, though, its focus has always been on China’s financial sector. That is the industry ripe for disruption, and where the money is. But as Ant and legions of investors have been reminded, that is also where the regulators lurk. ■

蚂蚁已经不遗余力地将自己打造成一家科技公司，而不是一家银行。它将其业务描述为“techfin”——即，将技术放在首位，而不是金融科技。在上市前，该公司要求券商指派科技分析师，而不仅仅是银行业分析师，来进行调查。不过，归根结底，它的重点始终是中国的金融业。这是一个颠覆的时机成熟的行业，也是钱所在的行业。但正如蚂蚁和大批投资者所提醒的那样，这也是监管者潜伏的地方。■

Apple exemplified an era of global capitalism that has passed

苹果代表了一个已经过去的全球资本主义时代

Apple’s next decade

苹果的下一个十年

![image-20210828102311730](/Users/chenyujie/Library/Application Support/typora-user-images/image-20210828102311730.png)

Tim Cook has to adapt to a new age of tech and globalisation

库克必须适应科技和全球化的新时代

WHEN TALKING about Apple, it is hard to avoid superlatives. It is the world’s most valuable firm, with a market value of $2.5trn. Over 80% of that has been amassed during the tenure of Tim Cook. No other chief executive has created more absolute value for shareholders. As he celebrates his tenth anniversary at the helm this week, he can look back with satisfaction. Instead of trying to imitate Apple’s co-founder, he took Steve Jobs’s creation and made it better and bigger. Much of that success has come from maintaining Apple’s record of innovation and its brand. But Mr Cook has also made the most of an era of open, globalised capitalism that is fading away. He plans to stay in charge for five years or more. How he deals with the new environment will form the next epic chapter in Apple’s story.

superlatives 最好的；最高级

amass 积聚

to be at the helm 掌舵

在谈论苹果时，难免会用到一些最高级的词。它是世界上最有价值的公司，其市值2.5万亿美元。其中超过80%财富是在库克任职期间积累的。没有哪位首席执行官比库克为股东创造的绝对价值更多。本周，当他庆祝自己作为掌舵人十周年时，他可以满意地回顾过去。他没有试图模仿苹果公司的联合创始人史蒂夫·乔布斯，而是借鉴了史蒂夫·乔布斯的创造，让它变得更好、更大。苹果的成功很大程度上来自于保持其创新的记录和品牌。但库克也充分利用了一个正在消失的开放、全球化的资本主义时代。他计划继续掌权5年或更长时间。他如何应对新环境将成为苹果故事的下一个史诗篇章。

Even by the standards of other tech giants, Apple is unusual. It is older (established in 1977); it mainly sells hardware; it is controlled by investors not founders; and it is more global, with a higher share of sales outside its home market than Alphabet, Amazon, Facebook, Microsoft, Alibaba or Tencent. Under Mr Cook’s supervision, it has exploited four trends. One is global supply chains: it has built an immense production network with China at the centre and components coming from around the world. This machine is being cranked up ahead of the launch of the new iPhone 13 next month, with unit sales of some 90m expected. As well as employing Chinese workers, Apple has made a killing from Chinese consumers, the second big trend. Its annual sales from China have roughly quintupled from a decade ago, to $60bn, more than those of any other Western firm.

crank up 调高

to quintuple sales 使销售量增加四倍

即使是以其他科技巨头的标准来看，苹果也是与众不同的。它的历史更悠久(成立于1977年); 它主要销售硬件; 它由投资者而不是创始人控制;它更加全球化，在本土市场以外的销售份额高于Alphabet、亚马逊、Facebook、微软、阿里巴巴或腾讯。在库克的监管下，该公司利用了四个趋势。其一是全球供应链:它建立了一个以中国为中心、零部件来自世界各地的巨大生产网络。在下月新iPhone 13发布之前，这台“机器”正在启动，预计单元销量将达到9000万左右。除了雇佣中国工人，苹果还从中国消费者那里大赚了一笔，这是第二大趋势。它在中国的年销售额大约是十年前的五倍，达到600亿美元，超过了其他任何西方公司。

Apple has also thrived in an era when governments were relaxed about firms with high market shares. While the handset sector used to be brutal (think of the rise and fall of BlackBerry), and is still very competitive for cheaper phones, at the high end Apple is ascendant, with a revenue market share of over 60% in America and a dominant position in operating systems there. Rather than compete with rival tech giants, it has been a beneficiary of a cartellish cosiness, receiving vast payments from Google in return for making it the iPhone’s search engine. The last trend is tax avoidance. Thanks in part to legal structures using tax havens, Apple has made average cash-income tax payments of just 17% of pre-tax profits over the past decade.

cartel: a group of similar independent companies who join together to control prices and limit competition

（多家公司为控制价格和限制竞争联合组成的）卡特尔，同业联盟

苹果也在一个政府对拥有高市场份额的公司持放松态度的时代蓬勃发展。虽然手机行业曾经是残酷的(想想黑莓的兴衰)，而且廉价手机仍然很有竞争力，但在高端市场上，苹果正在崛起，在美国的收入市场份额超过60%，在操作系统领域占据主导地位。它并没有与竞争对手科技巨头竞争，而是成为了垄断关系的受益者，从谷歌那里获得了巨额报酬，作为回报，让谷歌成为iPhone的搜索引擎。最后一个趋势是避税。在一定程度上，得益于使用避税天堂的法律结构，苹果在过去十年中平均支付的现金所得税仅占税前利润的17%。

![image-20210828102238626](/Users/chenyujie/Library/Application Support/typora-user-images/image-20210828102238626.png)

However, those four trends are becoming less favourable. Geopolitical tensions threaten global supply chains. President XX’s authoritarian policies have dulled the attractions of relying on XX consumers for 18% of sales. His new slogan of “common prosperity” may signal a desire to cut corporate profits. Western trustbusters are targeting tech, including those Google payments and Apple’s App Store, which Epic Games, the maker of “Fortnite”, accuses of levying excessive fees. And a deal this year brokered by the OECD may gradually force multinationals to pay more tax.

然而，这四个趋势正变得不那么有利。地缘政治紧张局势威胁着全球供应链。XX的专制政策削弱了依赖XX消费者获得的18%销售额的吸引力。他的新口号“XXXX”可能是削减企业利润的信号。西方反垄断者的目标是技术，包括谷歌支付和苹果的应用商店，《堡垒之夜》的制造商Epic Games指责苹果应用商店征收过高的费用。今年由经合组织斡旋达成的一项协议可能会逐渐迫使跨国公司缴纳更多的税。

So what, exactly, is Mr Cook’s plan? One of his achievements has been to maintain Apple’s cult of secrecy. Wall Street survives on a diet of generous share buybacks and meagre information about the firm’s strategy. Still, some things are clear.

a diet of sth 大量单一的某物

那么，库克先生的计划到底是什么呢? 他的成就之一就是保持了苹果对保密的狂热。华尔街靠慷慨的股票回购和关于公司战略的少量信息生存。不过，有些事情还是很清楚的。

Apple will find ways to sidestep tax bills, but the rate it pays will probably rise. It will continue to shift towards being a subscriber-based firm with over 1bn users who enjoy an array of services (which already generate 21% of sales). Apple is still about beautiful design and flawless manufacturing, but it also wants to be a trusted intermediary in a toxic and unruly digital sphere, able to charge handsome fees. And it will continue to try to invent a new generation of hardware—iGlasses or iCars, say—that can supplement the iPhone as the gateway to Apple’s world.

flawless 完美的

iGlasses for mac是一款非常强大的摄像头增强特效工具

苹果会找到避税的方法，但它支付的税率可能会上升。它将继续转变为一家以用户为基础的公司，其拥有超过10亿的用户，他们享受着一系列的服务(这已经产生了21%的销售额)。苹果的核心仍然是漂亮的设计和完美的制造，但它也希望在一个不良的且难控制的数字领域成为一个值得信任的中间人，能够收取可观的费用。它还将继续努力发明新一代的硬件，比如，iGlasses或iCars，作为iPhone的补充，作为进入苹果世界的门户。

Yet on the two thorniest problems Mr Cook has not made up his mind. On supply chains, although Apple has shifted the mix of its own long-term assets to America—the share has risen from 38% in 2012 to 70% now—key suppliers including TSMC, a chip firm, are lukewarm about putting production there. If the Sino-American rift deepens, or Apple’s relations with XX sour, Mr Cook will need to pivot away from XX, with momentous consequences for its margins and for world trade.

然而，对于这两个最棘手的问题，库克还没有做出决定。在供应链方面，尽管苹果已经将其长期资产的组合转移到美国，这一比例从2012年的38%上升到现在的70%，但包括芯片公司台积电在内的关键供应商对在美国生产并不感兴趣。如果中美之间的裂痕加深，或者苹果与XX方面的关系恶化，库克将需要将重心从XX转移出去，这将对苹果的利润率和世界贸易产生重大影响。

Meanwhile heat from trustbusters and Apple’s shift into services may catalyse competition with other tech firms. Apple has skirmished with Facebook over privacy this year; it could yet push deeper into search, e-commerce or entertainment, breaking apart tech’s cosy club. The rest of Mr Cook’s tenure is unlikely to be as successful as the first decade was, but his decisions will be just as momentous. ■

momentous 重大的

与此同时，反垄断者的热情和苹果向服务业的转变可能会促进与其他科技公司的竞争。今年，苹果与Facebook在隐私问题上发生了冲突;它还可能进一步进军搜索、电子商务或娱乐领域，打破科技行业的舒适俱乐部。库克剩下的任期不太可能像前十年那样成功，但他的决定将同样重要。■

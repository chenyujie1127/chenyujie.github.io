The origin of watermelons

西瓜的起源

![image-20210610112246985](/Users/chenyujie/Library/Application Support/typora-user-images/image-20210610112246985.png)

A popular fruit started off in Darfur

这个受欢迎的水果起源于达尔富尔

The origins of some crops are well known. Maize derives from a wild grass growing in the Balsas river valley, in what is now Mexico. Rice descends from another grass, native to the Yangzi basin(如果表示长江，应该是Yangtze吧). Potatoes hail from the border between Peru and Bolivia. Apples trace back to the woodlands of southern Kazakhstan. Some crops’ beginnings, though, are lost in the mists of time— among them those of the watermelon.

maize 玉米

descend from 起源于

be native to somewhere 原产于某地

basin 流域

hail from 来自

trace back to 追溯到

woodland 树林

be lost in the mists of time 湮没在时间的迷雾之中

一些作物的起源是众所周知的。玉米起源于一种生长在巴尔萨斯河谷的一种野草，巴尔萨斯河谷位于现在的墨西哥。水稻起源于长江流域的另一种草。土豆产自秘鲁和玻利维亚的边界。苹果最早可以追溯到哈萨克斯坦南部的林地。不过，有些作物的起源会消失在时间的迷雾中， 西瓜就是其中之一。

That watermelons’ ancestors are African has long been clear. Archaeological evidence from Libya and Egypt suggests they were cultivated there thousands of years ago, and the continent is home to seven species and numerous subspecies of plants classified in the same genus, Citrullus, as the cultivated crop. But only now has a likely candidate been nailed down. An examination of available genetic data about members of Citrullus, published in the Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences by Susanne Renner of Washington University, in St Louis, and Guillaume Chomicki of the University of Sheffield, in Britain, has led them to conclude that watermelons were domesticated from a subspecies called the Sudanese Kordofan melon, which grows in Darfur, the western part of Sudan.

archaeological 考古学的

subspecies 亚种

Citrullus 西瓜属

nail down 确定

domesticate 驯化

植物驯化： 每一种植物你看介绍的时候都会有原产地和分布范围。原产地的植物被带到另一个地区或另一个环境中生长，使其适应于新的环境，在适应新环境的过程中植物某些特征会发生变化，这些变化就是植物驯化产生的效果。

众所周知，西瓜的祖先是非洲人。来自利比亚和埃及的考古证据表明，他们早在几千年前就在那里种植过，这块大陆还是7种植物和与栽培的作物同属,即西瓜属的许多亚种植物的发源地。_20210610_102014但直到现在才确定了一个可能的候选人。圣路易斯华盛顿大学的Susanne Renner和英国谢菲尔德大学的Guillaume Chomicki在《美国国家科学院院刊》(PNAS)上发表了一项关于西瓜属植物现有基因数据的研究，研究人员得出结论，西瓜是由苏丹西部达尔富尔地区的苏丹科尔多凡瓜亚种驯化而来的。

Tellingly, this is one of the few wild members of Citrullus that is bland, rather than excruciatingly bitter to the human palate. That ties in with a reinterpretation by the two researchers of a 4,450­ year-old Egyptian tomb painting (pictured). The previous assumption had been that early cultivated watermelons were too bitter to eat raw, and would thus need to be cooked and sweetened for consumption. This painting, though, shows what appears to be a stripped watermelon being served raw at a table decorated by lotus flowers.

tellingly 有力地；透露真相地

excruciatingly 及其；十分

palate 味觉

tie sth in with sth 将某事物同某事物相结合; 将某物同某物相连接

值得一提的是，这是为数不多的野生西瓜属植物之一，它的味道很清淡，而不是让人难以忍受的苦味。这与两位研究人员对一幅有4450年历史的埃及古墓绘画的重新解读有关。先前的假设是，早期栽培的西瓜太苦，不能生吃，因此需要煮熟加糖才能食用。然而，这幅画显示的是在一个装饰着莲花的桌子上，有一个被剥了皮的生西瓜。

Nicola Sturgeon’s resignation is part of Britain’s great moderation

Peak populism

民粹主义巅峰

Pragmatism is taking hold north and south of the border

实用主义在边境的南北两边都很盛行

Feb 15th 2023

The image of Britain as a land of phlegmatic common sense has taken a beating in the past ten years. This is a country that voted to leave the European Union without any coherent plan to make the best of it; whose two main parties offered the electorate the choice of Boris Johnson or Jeremy Corbyn in 2019; and whose union has frayed in the face of successful nationalist movements in Scotland and Northern Ireland.

在过去的十年里，英国作为一个冷静而有常识的国家的形象受到了打击。这个国家投票决定离开欧盟，却没有任何连贯的计划来充分利用这一点; 2019年，英国的两个主要政党让选民在鲍里斯·约翰逊或杰里米·科尔宾之间做出选择; 面对苏格兰和北爱尔兰成功的民族主义运动，它的联盟出现了裂痕。

If one definition of populism is offering simplistic answers to complex questions, British politics has suffered as bad a case as anywhere in the rich world. Now, though, the pendulum is swinging back. The decision of Nicola Sturgeon, Scotland’s first minister, to resign as leader of the Scottish Nationalist Party (snp) is the latest evidence that Britain is rediscovering the virtues of moderation.

如果民粹主义的一个定义是为复杂的问题提供简单的答案，那么英国政治遭受的痛苦不亚于任何发达国家。然而现在，钟摆又摆回来了。苏格兰首席大臣尼古拉·斯特金决定辞去苏格兰民族党领袖的职务，这是英国重新发现稳定优点的最新证据。

Ms Sturgeon has devoted her working life to the goal of Scottish independence. That is a legitimate political cause, though not one this newspaper supports, and she has been a talented advocate for it. Her failure to achieve it partly reflects the snp’s poor record of running Scotland’s devolved government. But it is also explained by the intemperance of her tactics.

斯特金女士把她的职业生涯都献给了苏格兰独立的目标。这是一项合法的政治事业，尽管本刊并不支持，而她一直是这一事业的天才倡导者。她未能实现这一目标，在一定程度上反映了苏格兰民族党在管理苏格兰权力下放政府方面的糟糕记录。但这也可以用她的无节制策略来解释。

She has taken to calling the barriers that prevent the snp from holding a second referendum on independence a “democratic outrage”. In fact, they are part of the constitution and have been upheld by the judiciary. She has proposed making the next election a de facto ballot on independence, with each vote for the snp counting as a vote to break away. On her watch, attitudes to independence have become a deeply tribal matter.

她已开始将阻止苏格兰民族党举行第二次独立公投的障碍称为“民主暴行”。事实上，它们是宪法的一部分，并得到了司法部门的支持。她提议让下一次选举成为事实上的独立投票，投给苏格兰民族党的每一票都被算作是投给独立的一票。在她的任期内，对独立的态度已经成为一个深刻的部落问题。

With her resignation, that approach seems to have run its course. Polling suggests that Scottish voters are twice as likely to disapprove of Ms Sturgeon as approve of her. They remain unpersuaded of the case for independence and don’t much like the idea of a single-issue election. In her valedictory speech Ms Sturgeon herself seemed to recognise that polarisation imposes a ceiling on support for independence, as well as a floor.

随着她的辞职，这种做法似乎已经走到了尽头。民调显示，苏格兰选民反对斯特金的可能性是支持她的两倍。他们仍然不相信独立，也不太喜欢单一议题选举的想法。在斯特金的告别演讲中，她自己似乎也认识到，两极分化对支持独立设定了上限，也设定了下限。

South of the border, too, moderation is in vogue. On the same day that Ms Sturgeon announced she was quitting, Sir Keir Starmer, the leader of the Labour Party, confirmed that Mr Corbyn would not be allowed to stand as a Labour candidate in the next election, which is due to take place by January 2025. That sets a symbolic seal on Sir Keir’s overhaul of the opposition since taking over in 2020, whereby he has systematically purged the hard left from the party apparatus. Mr Corbyn wanted to smash capitalism; Sir Keir swans around Davos.

在边境以南，稳定也很流行。就在斯特金宣布辞职的同一天，工党领袖基尔·斯塔默爵士证实，科尔宾将不被允许作为工党候选人参加定于2025年1月举行的下届选举。这为基尔爵士自2020年上任以来对反对党的全面改革奠定了象征性的基础，他在此期间系统地清除了党内的强硬左翼分子。科尔宾想要粉碎资本主义; 基尔爵士在达沃斯游弋。

The Tories are less disciplined than Labour but they, too, have become more moderate. Rishi Sunak is a reaction to Mr Johnson and Liz Truss. His pitch is based on managerial competence rather than cartoonish radicalism. As public support for Brexit wanes, he is building bridges with the eu. Ms Truss wanted to dynamite orthodoxy; Mr Sunak is keen to work with institutions. The choice facing the British electorate next time will almost certainly be between two uncharismatic pragmatists with great hair—the opposite of Mr Johnson.

保守党没有工党那么有纪律，但他们也变得更加稳定。里希·苏纳克是对约翰逊和利兹·特拉斯的回应。他的宣传语是基于管理能力，而不是卡通式的激进主义。随着公众对英国脱欧的支持减弱，他正在与欧盟建立桥梁。特拉斯女士想要打破正统; 苏纳克热衷于与机构合作。下一次英国选民面临的选择几乎肯定会是在两个毫无魅力的、有着一头秀发的实用主义者（与约翰逊相反）之间。

If populism has peaked, however, it is far from dead. Brexiteers can still cause immense damage; the battle to succeed Ms Sturgeon may throw up another divisive snp leader. And moderation carries dangers of its own: the gradualist approach is not sufficient to meet the challenge of Britain’s woeful productivity or to overhaul its creaking public services. But a more rational form of politics is taking hold, in which competence matters more than ideology, problems demand policies rather than scapegoats and a cause like Scottish independence is advanced by good governance rather than grandstanding. ■

然而，即便民粹主义已见顶，它也远未消亡。脱欧派仍可能造成巨大损害; 继任斯特金的斗争可能会产生另一位分裂的苏格兰民族党领袖。节制本身也有危险: 渐进的方法不足以应对英国糟糕的生产力所带来的挑战，也不足以彻底改革其摇摇欲坠的公共服务。但一种更理性的政治形式正在生根发芽，在这种政治形式中，能力比意识形态更重要，问题需要政策而不是替罪羊，苏格兰独立这样的事业是通过良好的治理而不是哗众取宠来推进的。■

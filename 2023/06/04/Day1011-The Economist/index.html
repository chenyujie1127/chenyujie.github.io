The widespread adoption of AI by companies will take a while

企业普遍采用人工智能需要一段时间

AI and productivity

人工智能和生产力

![image-20230702093605330](/Users/chenyujie/Library/Application Support/typora-user-images/image-20230702093605330.png)

For the technology to make its mark on productivity, even mediocre firms will need to make the leap

为了让科技在生产力上有所作为，即使是普通的公司也需要做出改变

image: travis constantine

The 2010s brought no shortage of miraculous technologies, from tablet computers and 4g mobile internet to new forms of artificial intelligence (ai)—Hey, Siri! But these had surprisingly little effect on the economy. During that decade productivity growth in the rich world averaged a measly 1% a year, holding down average wages. Innovative firms embraced new tech, but many less adventurous ones did not bother, and saw few efficiency gains as a result. The experience showed that technological wizardry and improvements in average living standards do not always go hand in hand.

2010年代带来了许多神奇的技术，从平板电脑和4G移动互联网到新形式的人工智能 -嘿，Siri！但令人惊讶的是，这些技术对经济的影响微乎其微。在那十年里，发达国家的生产率平均每年仅增长1% ，这压低了平均工资。创新的公司拥抱了新技术，但许多不那么冒险的公司却不愿为此费心，结果效率几乎没有提高。经验表明，技术奇迹和平均生活水平的提高并不总是齐头并进的。

Generative ai, its boosters say, will be different. Not since the invention of the internet has a new technology so captured the public imagination. The technology is consumer-friendly: within days of its release to the public, Chatgpt, the most famous ai chatbot, had millions of users. It is easy to see how this innovation could improve all types of work at all types of firms, from increasing the accuracy of doctors’ diagnoses to helping programmers write software code more efficiently.

生成式人工智能的支持者表示这项技术将有所不同。自互联网发明以来，还没有一项新技术如此引发了公众的无限遐想。这项技术对消费者很友好:最著名的人工智能聊天机器人Chatgpt在向公众发布后的几天内就拥有了数百万用户。从提高医生诊断的准确性到帮助程序员更高效编写软件代码，很容易看出这种创新是如何改善所有类型公司的所有类型的工作。

Some companies are already incorporating ai into their operations. Tech firms are investing heavily in the technology, advertising for many thousands of roles. So are some bricks-and-mortar companies. A drug discovered and designed by ai is progressing through human trials in China. Analysts at ubs reckon that Domino’s Pizza can use ai to “improve the accuracy of order-delivery-time estimates”. Investors are rewarding the early adopters. Since the start of the year, the median share price of the most ai-enthusiastic firms in the s&p 500 has risen by 11%. For those moving more slowly, it has not changed at all.

一些公司已经将人工智能融入到他们的业务中。科技公司正在大力投资这项技术，公开招聘数千个岗位。一些实体公司也是如此。一种由人工智能发现和设计的药物正在中国进行人体试验。瑞银分析师认为，达美乐披萨可以利用人工智能“提高订单交货时间估算的准确性”。投资者正在“奖励”早期采用者。自今年年初以来，标普500指数中最热衷人工智能的公司的股价中值上涨了11%。对于那些行动缓慢的公司来说，股价则完全没有变化。

The potential is huge. Yet for ai to truly diffuse through the economy, it needs to make its mark beyond the most go-getting companies. And this will take time. Although the internet began to be used by some companies in the early 1990s, it was not until the late 2000s that two-thirds of American businesses had a website. Some 70 firms in the s&p 500 still show no interest in ai, according to our analysis. And below the corporate crème de la crème, the trends look even less encouraging. According to one recent survey of American and Canadian firms, a third of small businesses have no firm plans to try generative-ai tools over the next year. Some evidence even suggests that usage of Chatgpt and its competitors is falling—perhaps as people have tried it out, and then decided it is not for them.

人工智能的潜力是巨大的。然而，人工智能要想真正渗透到整个经济中，它需要在最积极进取的公司之外留下印记。这需要时间。虽然互联网在20世纪90年代初开始被一些公司使用，但直到21世纪后期，三分之二的美国企业才拥有网站。根据我们的分析，标普500指数中约有70家公司仍对人工智能不感兴趣。而在“精英”企业之下，趋势看起来更不令人鼓舞。根据最近对美国和加拿大公司的一项调查，三分之一的小企业没有在明年尝试生成人工智能工具的明确计划。一些证据甚至表明，Chatgpt及其竞争对手的使用率正在下降——也许是因为人们试用了它，然后认为它不适合他们。

Can ai live up to its promise? Organisations like the oecd propose lots of ways to improve diffusion from the best firms to the rest, including through better education, schemes to raise business investment and changes to competition policy. Such goals are worthy, but hard to achieve. Efforts by technology firms to make ai cheaper and easier to use will do more to speed up adoption. In practice, most companies will adopt ai by default, as new, ai-powered features are added to the software and services that they already use.

人工智能能实现它的承诺吗？像经合组织这样的组织提出了许多方法来改善从最好的公司向其他公司的传播，包括通过更好的教育、增加商业投资的计划和改变竞争政策。这些目标是有价值的，但很难实现。科技公司努力使人工智能更便宜、更容易使用，这将更有助于加速人工智能的普及。实际上，大多数公司将默认采用人工智能，因为他们已经在使用的软件和服务中添加了新的人工智能功能。

Indeed, even the most powerful technologies take time to diffuse, because companies tend to use a hotch-potch of software and services, some of which may be years or even decades old. Replacing outdated systems can be costly, complicated and painful. Moreover, in the many industries either run or heavily regulated by the government, such as health care, education and construction, bosses and trade unions often resist the deployment of new technology, worried that it will lead to job losses. In time ai could well transform how people live their lives and do their jobs. But the road to widespread diffusion, and any resulting productivity boom, will be a long one. ■

确实，即使是最强大的技术也需要时间来传播，因为公司倾向于使用大杂烩的软件和服务，其中一些可能已经有几年甚至几十年的历史了。更换过时的系统成本高昂、复杂且痛苦。此外，在许多由政府经营或严格监管的行业，如医疗、教育和建筑，老板和工会经常抵制部署新技术，他们担心这会导致失业。假以时日，人工智能很可能会改变人们的生活和工作方式。但是，通往广泛传播以及由此带来的生产力繁荣的道路将是漫长的。■
